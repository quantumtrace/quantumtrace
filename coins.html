Tutorial: Quantum Randon Number Generator.

The first step is to initialiaze a 2 qubit quantum register and a 2 bit classical register.

Then we need to apply a Hadamard gate to all qubits. This gate put a qubit in to a superposition of 0 and 1 with equal probability.

After this we measure every qubit and this measurement will collapse the qubit to a 0 or a 1.
